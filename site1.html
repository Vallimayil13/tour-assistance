Brihadeeswarar Temple (Big Temple)


Brihadeeswarar Temple in Thanjavur is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the largest temples in India. It is renowned for its grand architecture, with a towering 66-meter-high central dome, one of the tallest in the world. The temple features intricate carvings, stunning sculptures, and a majestic gopuram (gateway tower). Its design and structure showcase the brilliance of Dravidian art and engineering. The temple is an important cultural landmark and a popular pilgrimage destination.

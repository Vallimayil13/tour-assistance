Nilgiri Mountain Railway


The Nilgiri Mountain Railway (NMR) is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that operates a scenic 46 km route from Mettupalayam to Ooty. It traverses the Nilgiri Hills, offering breathtaking views of forests, valleys, and tea plantations. The railway is famous for its historic steam locomotives and the unique cogwheel system used to navigate steep gradients. The journey provides a nostalgic experience, showcasing early 20th-century engineering amidst stunning landscapes. As both a cultural and tourist attraction, the NMR remains a vital part of the region's heritage and charm.
